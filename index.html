The Place

Barefoot on the outside. Considered on the inside.

On Board sits directly on Libaong's shoreline in Panglao, a short tricycle ride from Alona's restaurant strip and Bohol's dive shops. We built the resort around the idea that a great beach stay needs almost nothing extra — just a solid bed, a private stretch of sand, and somewhere cold to put your feet up at sunset.

Every booking here is handled directly with our front desk, so the rate you lock in below is the rate you pay — no third-party markups, no surprise resort fees at checkout.
