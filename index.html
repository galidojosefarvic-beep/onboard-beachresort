The Hostel & Resort

A place to arrive and belong

On Board Panglao Beach Hostel and Resort sits along the shoreline of Libaong, Panglao — a place where the line between hostel and resort blurs into something better. Private beach access, a buzzing bar, and rooms that range from budget-friendly to comfortable private stays.

Whether you're a solo traveler looking for new friends or a couple seeking a quiet beachfront escape, On Board Panglao offers the best of both worlds — social energy when you want it, and peace when you need it.
